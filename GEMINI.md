# Project: Capital Cabinetry & Assembly / Ébénisterie et Assemblage de la Capitale

## Business Profile
- **Names:**
  - English: Capital Cabinetry & Assembly
  - French: Ébénisterie et Assemblage de la Capitale
- **Location:** Ottawa / Gatineau (National Capital Region)
- **Mission:** High-quality cabinetry installation, IKEA assembly, and general handyman services (TV mounts, shelving) for the NCR, leveraging AI to streamline leads and marketing while maintaining a human-first bilingual experience.
- **Priority:** Cash jobs and high-urgency assembly/mounting.

## Tech Stack & Tooling
- **Marketing:** ComfyUI (AI Image Enhancement), ElevenLabs (Bilingual Voiceover), Canva/AI-assisted design (Flyers).
- **Lead Generation:** OpenClaw/Claude API for monitoring Kijiji, Marketplace, and Craigslist.
- **Website:** Astro/Next.js (Bilingual, Static) hosted on GitHub Pages or Netlify.
- **CRM:** Simple local job tracker/portfolio manager.

## Core Services
1.  **Full Cabinetry:** Kitchen/Bath installs and custom modifications.
2.  **IKEA & Flat-Pack:** Rapid assembly for IKEA, Wayfair, etc.
3.  **Mounting & Handyman:** TV mounting, shelving, and art hanging.

## Important Notes for Future AI Instances
- **Bilingualism is mandatory:** All public-facing content (flyers, website, lead drafts) MUST be in both English and French.
- **Human-First Communication:** AI is used for *drafting* only. The user reviews and sends all communications.
- **Cash Flow Priority:** Focus lead generation on immediate-need tasks (IKEA assembly, TV mounts) to maintain high liquid cash flow.
- **Visuals:** Portfolio images should be high-res and optimized for web performance.
